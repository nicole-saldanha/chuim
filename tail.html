TAILORING

Sewing classes commenced to offer women an opportunity for self help or to work as assistants in tailoring shops. This was also an attempt to provide vocational training for women.After the completion of course, students appear for the exam and they are awarded with the certificate. There are two batches from 2pm -4pm and 4pm-6pm. There is a syllabus to follow which range from small kids dress to fashion designing for adults.

Center had received to make permanent deacon stole by Health Promotion Trust and the work was appreciated by the deacon.
