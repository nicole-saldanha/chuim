CRECHE

Children from 6 months to 3 years are admitted to the creche. Mothers who keep their babies in the creche are mostly domestic workers, fisher folks and those who live a hand to mouth existence. In many cases, the women have to care for their families single handedly due to separation or alcoholic or unemployed husbands. The three care-takers, with their supervisor reach out to these small children with much love and care. The creche has a spacious hall, well equipped with toys and other play-materials. There is CD player which helps teacher to make atmosphere conducive for learning .Apart from these there are extracurricular activities like Games, Dance and learning ABCD for above two and half year children.

Why select this crèches?
-> There is conducive atmosphere in the crèches for the child’s overall growth. There is space for children to play, sing and dance.

-> The children are fed with Nutritiousfood prepared from the Menu. Maintaining hygiene and cleanliness and health care.

-> Monthly parents meeting, birthday celebrations,Childrens’ Day celebrations and various /games are encouraged in crèche.

-> The Children wear uniform which identify them with the centre -for girls red colour and boys blue.

-> There are other child care centers but compared to them; the services which are provided and the care undertaken makes the parents and children to come to our Chuim Centre.





BALWADI

Balwadi is an Indian pre-school run for economically weaker sections of the society, either by government or NGOs. Chuim Community Centre has been giving their services, by providing crèche facility from 6 months to 3 year old babies. From June, 2017 centre has started new Balwadi (Kids Villa) activity for age group 3 years to 4 years. Here they are getting formal and informal education and also nutrition food. The objectives of the Balwadi is to give children a growing environment. There is a class teacher as well as there are four volunteer teachers for skill development like music, exercise, poetry and memory
